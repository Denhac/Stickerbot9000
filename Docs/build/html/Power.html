Power¶

Power On¶

  1. Remove lock and open case.
  2. Switch power strip on.
  3. Power on printer.
    1. Remove wiring harness from between printer and bill collector.
    2. Slide printer backwards until power button on top of printer is visible.
    3. Push the power button.
    4. Verify the “Status” light is lit.
    5. Slide printer forwards into position. Verify printer engages sticker dispenser ramp.
    6. Replace wiring harness between printer and bill collector.
  4. Power on EL lighting.
    1. Push button on inverter installed just behind power strip.
    2. Each press cycles through to a different mode.
      1. Solid on.
      2. Slow flash.
      3. Fast flash.
      4. Off
  5. Close case and replace lock.

Power Off¶

  1. Remove lock and open case.
  2. Turn off switch on power strip.
  3. Close case and replace lock.
