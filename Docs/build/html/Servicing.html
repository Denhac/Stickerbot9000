Servicing¶

You didn’t think Stickerbot 9000 would make you rich without any work did you? Luckily you don’t have to do much.

Replacing Sticker Roll¶

  1. Remove lock and open case.
  2. Remove wiring harness from between printer and bill collector.
  3. Slide printer backwards until top of printer is visible.
  4. Open top of printer and replace sticker roll.
  5. Close top of printer.
  6. Verify the “Status” light is lit. Press power button if light is not lit.
  7. Slide printer forwards into position. Verify printer engages sticker dispenser ramp.
  8. Replace wiring harness between printer and bill collector.
  9. Close case and replace lock.

Collecting Your Cash¶

  1. Remove lock and open case.
  2. Remove wiring harness from between printer and bill collector.
  3. Remove bill collector by rotating bottom toward you until feed clears case.
  4. Slide release on bottom of bill hopper toward rear to unlock.
  5. Slide bill hopper toward top of unit to remove.
  6. Access door on side of bill hopper to remove money.
  7. Replace bill hopper and slide down to lock.
  8. Replace bill collector by sliding forward into place in case.
  9. Replace wiring harness between printer and bill collector.
  10. Close case and replace lock.
